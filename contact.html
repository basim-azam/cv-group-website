Join Our Group

We are always looking for motivated students and researchers to join our group. If you are interested in computer vision, machine learning, or related areas, please consider the following opportunities:

PhD Positions

We have openings for PhD students interested in:

  • Adversarial machine learning and model robustness
  • Explainable and interpretable AI
  • 3D point cloud processing and understanding
  • Responsible generative AI
  • Multi-modal foundation models
  • Medical image analysis

Applicants should have:

  • Strong background in computer science, mathematics, or related field
  • Experience with deep learning frameworks (PyTorch, TensorFlow)
  • Good programming skills (Python)
  • Publications in top venues are a plus

To apply, please send your CV, transcripts, and a brief research statement to naveed.akhtar1@unimelb.edu.au.

Postdoctoral Positions

Postdoctoral positions may be available depending on funding. Please contact us with your CV and research interests.

Visiting Researchers

We welcome visiting students and researchers for collaborative projects. Please reach out if you are interested in a research visit.

Scholarships

Information about scholarships for international students can be found at the University of Melbourne Graduate Research Scholarships page.
