We are always looking for talented and motivated individuals to join our research group. If you are passionate about computer vision, machine learning, and AI, we encourage you to explore the opportunities below.

## Current Openings

### PhD Positions

We have openings for PhD students interested in:

- Adversarial machine learning and AI security
- Explainable and interpretable AI
- 3D point cloud analysis and scene understanding
- Vision-language models
- Medical image analysis

**Requirements:**
- Strong academic background (First Class Honours or Masters degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field)
- Programming experience in Python, PyTorch/TensorFlow
- Strong mathematical foundation
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills
- Publications in top venues (preferred but not required)

**Funding:**
Competitive scholarships are available through the University of Melbourne Graduate Research Scholarships and other funding sources.

### Masters Research Projects

We offer research projects for Masters students enrolled in relevant programs at the University of Melbourne. Projects are available in all our research areas.

### Visiting Researchers

We welcome visiting PhD students, postdocs, and researchers for collaborative research stays. Duration typically ranges from 3 months to 1 year.

## What We Offer

- **Excellent research environment**: Access to state-of-the-art computing facilities and a vibrant research community
- **Strong mentorship**: Regular one-on-one meetings, feedback on research and writing, career guidance
- **Collaboration opportunities**: Work with leading researchers nationally and internationally
- **Publication support**: Guidance on publishing at top venues (CVPR, ICCV, ECCV, NeurIPS, etc.)
- **Professional development**: Opportunities to attend conferences, workshops, and summer schools

## How to Apply

### For PhD Positions

1. **Review our research**: Familiarize yourself with our recent publications and research areas
2. **Prepare your materials**:
   - CV/Resume
   - Academic transcripts
   - Research statement (1-2 pages describing your research interests and potential topics)
   - Writing sample (publication or thesis chapter)
   - Contact details for 2-3 academic references

3. **Email your application**: Send your materials to [naveed.akhtar1@unimelb.edu.au](mailto:naveed.akhtar1@unimelb.edu.au) with subject line: "PhD Application - [Your Name]"

4. **Formal application**: If invited, submit a formal application through the [University of Melbourne Graduate Research portal](https://study.unimelb.edu.au/how-to-apply/graduate-research)

### For Masters Projects

Contact Dr. Akhtar with your CV and a brief description of your research interests. Please also indicate which specific research area(s) interest you.

### For Visiting Positions

Send an email with:
- Your CV
- Current research description
- Proposed duration and dates
- Funding status (self-funded or seeking support)
- Potential collaboration topics

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What is the typical PhD duration?**
A: PhD programs at the University of Melbourne typically take 3-4 years full-time.

**Q: Do I need a Masters degree to apply for a PhD?**
A: A Masters degree is preferred but not required. Exceptional candidates with Honours degrees and relevant research experience will be considered.

**Q: What programming skills are expected?**
A: Strong Python programming is essential. Experience with PyTorch or TensorFlow is highly desirable. Experience with other tools (Git, Linux, HPC systems) is beneficial.

**Q: Can international students apply?**
A: Yes, we welcome applications from qualified candidates worldwide. Note that international students must meet English language requirements.
