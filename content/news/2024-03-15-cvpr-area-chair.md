---
title: "Dr. Akhtar Serves as Area Chair for CVPR 2024"
date: 2024-03-15
tags: ["Service"]
---

Dr. Naveed Akhtar has been invited to serve as an Area Chair for CVPR 2024.

CVPR (IEEE/CVF Conference on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition) is the premier annual conference in computer vision, featuring cutting-edge research from around the world.

As an Area Chair, Dr. Akhtar will oversee the review process for submitted papers in his area of expertise, ensuring high-quality and fair evaluation of research contributions.

This continues Dr. Akhtar's record of service to the research community, having previously served as Area Chair for ECCV and WACV.
