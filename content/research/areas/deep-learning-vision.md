We conduct foundational research in deep learning for computer vision, developing novel architectures, training methods, and representation learning techniques that advance the state of the art.

## Research Focus

- **Visual representation learning**: Learning effective features from visual data
- **Vision transformers**: Applying attention mechanisms to visual understanding
- **Video understanding**: Temporal modeling for action recognition and tracking
- **Vision-language models**: Connecting visual and textual understanding

## Contributions

Our fundamental research contributes to advancing the core capabilities of computer vision systems, enabling new applications and improving performance across diverse visual tasks.
